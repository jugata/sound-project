Synth In C

Welcome to Synth In C. Inspired by Terry Riley's 1964 musical composition In C, you can explore and perform a miniaturized version of this masterpiece alone, or with others.

For an in-depth look at how the original piece is performed, check out this wiki page. Otherwise, follow these simple rules:

  1. Click Start/Pulse
  2. Activate Cell 1
  3. Continue to activate the cells in order, but have no more than 3-4 adjacent cells active at a time. Take your time progressing through the cells.
  4. The piece will end the way it began, with just the Pulse.
Note: If playing with multiple performers/devices, there should be only one Pulse activated.
